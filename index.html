Filippo Diara

Archaeologist and Ph.D in Architectural and Landscape Heritage

Postdoctoral Research Fellow (ICAR/06) - Politecnico di Torino; Management Assistant for the ERASMUS+ project ERAMCA (Environmental risk assessment and mitigation on Cultural Heritage assets in Central Asia); G4CH (Geomatics For Cultural Heritage) Laboratory member - DAD department POLITO. His research activity is about documentation of Cultural Heritage assets, especially 3D modeling and open source HBIM solutions applied to archaeology and building archaeology. He is author of +15 scientific papers both in national and international reviews as well as short novels.
